Mitchell Craft

Technical 3D Artist, Animator & VFX Artist
Based in New York City

Mitchell Craft is a Technical 3D Artist, Animator, and VFX Artist working across game development, fashion, music, and virtual production. He brings a multidisciplinary approach to every project — from building procedural pipeline tools to creating large-scale visual effects for global brands and artists.

His work has appeared in campaigns for Miu Miu and Moncler, arena experiences for the LA Kings, and music videos for Megan Thee Stallion and Bring Me The Horizon, among others. He holds an MFA in Digital Art from Tulane University, where his thesis explored generative art-making at the intersection of real-time 3D and machine learning.
